You walk up to the door and knock on it three times. After several seconds, the door opens to reveal an old man.
He says something to you, but you don't understand what he is saying. The old man realizes this and snaps his fingers.
This time you understand him asking,

"What are you doing here, my starchy friend?"

You make gestures to signify that you are looking for a place to grow. The old man smiles and says,

"Well, I do have a garden where you can grow, but I think I have a solution you might like better..."

He tells you to wait there while he goes to get something.
Minutes later, he arrives with a staff and points it at you, chanting something you don't understand.
All of a sudden, you feel a tingling sensation, then you start to grow and your body starts changing.

You've become a human.

Once the transformation completes, the old man looks at you in satisfaction and says,

"Why not come in and have a drink?"

You enter his house, excited about what's to come next.
