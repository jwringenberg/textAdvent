Danny allows you to ride him, and together you two continue into Memori.

From glen to glen, and down the mountain side, the summer's gone and all the flowers dying. 'Tis you, 'tis you must go and I must bide.

You look forward and see a younger version of yourself, playing in a snowy forrest. A horn blows in the distance, and a far away army begins to storm forward-about to trample your younger self.

And I will her tho' soft you tread above me. And then my grave will warm and sweeter be.
