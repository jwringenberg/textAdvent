When you swing the sword the blade pierces though the dragons neck causing the beast to lose it's head.

When you peer down at the sword you see that it has appeared to grow dragon like qualities like dragon hide hilt and dragon scales for the guard.

You have slain the dragon and loot all of it's treasure you live your life as an adventurer and are now known as the DragonSlayer and you are granted a small kingdom to rule.
Your Kingdom becomes the most powerful in the lands and you eventually pass your treasure and kingdom to your heir.
