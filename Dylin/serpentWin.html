Serpent!

You start down your chosen path. After several minutes of walking, you come to a gate of rusted steel. Seeing the gate, you know you must be close to the crown. As you approach the gate to inspect it, you hear shuffling behind you. You spin around, and see a serpent towering over you! An epic battle ensues, and both you and the monster are covered in cuts and bruises. Finally, after fighting for what feels like eternity, the you finally managed to behead the serpent with your sword.


The gate opens behind you. You must be close to the crown.
